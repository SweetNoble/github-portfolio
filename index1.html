About the Tribe

The Tribe of Judah is a religious group under The New Wine Ministries, named after Jacob's son whose name means "praise."
Marked by leadership, courage, and redemption, Judah’s line brought forth King David and Jesus Christ.
As a church group, we carry this legacy — called to lead with faith and live lives that honor God.
